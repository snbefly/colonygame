ARCHITECTURAL EYE

When you take some time to plan out the exploration of a new ruin, roll +Steel. On a 7-9 hold 2; on a 10+ hold 3. While you explore the ruin, spend your hold 1-for-1 to:

  • Roll +Steel for Forge a Path instead of +Force.
  • Identify and track a danger within the ruin.
  • Find a way to use the ruin as a weapon.
