First Contact

0 years after contact

The moment of the discovery of the new species split the calendar in two, we were treading on someone else's homeland. The landlords of the forests and mountains. Our people called them Druids in reference to an old folklore legend. But the first contact ended up in a misunderstanding that resulted in the loss of life for both sides. Now an old archaeological quarry stays there as the memorial, appropriately called Point of Contact

Touchdown

3 years before contact

The first modules rain down onto the surface. Hardy trained scouts are spreading across the surface accompanied by their MULEs. The monolithic structure hanging in the air overseeing this all is a mutli-purpose high orbit space station Transtar that controls the operation. Life funnels from all across the sector to try their luck in the new frontier. And so it begins...
