Optima

Our new home

The planet is in the last stages of ecosystem formation, this is an ideal opportunity to study and integrate civilization into its circulation. Unfortunately, this also means that the planet has bared its teeth and is ready to bite. The climate, although acceptable, is extremely contrasting from biome to biome, and the atmosphere, although is similar enough to breathe, not for very long. Volcanic activity is in the withering phase, which means that a huge amount of deposits and minerals have just formed not so deep below the surface.

Notable Locations

Nexus - is the middle section of the ship. The rotation gravity allows Transtar to plaster the inside surface of the cynindrical structure with roads and buildings, which makes the view stunning as your sense of gravity evaporates looking at people walking head down above you and skyscrapers connecting the two sides.

Backdrop locations

Town hall, residential dome slowly devolving into slums, fusion reactor, farming dome, the co-op bazar of everything, array of backup solar panels and wind turbines along the coast
