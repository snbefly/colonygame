The Drilling Facility

The furnace that stokes our future

The Drilling Facility is a truly big complex, sealed and protected from environment, it is the place of labour that brings the colony its needed construction and manufactuing resources. Placed on top of a Iron deposits (and a diverse but small percentage of other mixed ores), with clay-like fertilized soil. Was initiall working under the administration of Transtar Inc., in summer 2 year a.c. it was transfered to a labour union, which formed under pressure of bad working conditions and possible health and safety violations.

Backdrop locations

Large storage halls with cargo walkers and magnetic cranes, maze slim maintenance tunnels, administration office block, wet and dirty mining tunnels.
