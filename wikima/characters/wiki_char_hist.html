Origin

He has many names, Butcher of Dalih, Joke the Ripper, Neon Drifter, Undead Anacrhist, Pot... But most people who know him well enough to be around and feel safe call him Hist. He is armed and dangerous criminal with unknown origins and motives, moved apparently just by his thirst for blood, gore and violence, leaving most of his victims scorched and teared apart, not uncommonly filled with bite marks. He has an bounty on his head and a big criminal record, starging with acts of industrial terrorism including underground factories exploiting floran slaves for profit where he almoust died, but records showed he was saved amoung other florans by mistirious blue floran lady. Its unclear who he really works for and what he wants. But its clear he is vicious killer and a hunter.

ANARCHIST’S COOKBOOK

With a few hours and access to volatile chemicals, you can make 1-stock of explosives. 1-stock is sufficient to:

  • Spread fire over a city block-sized area.
  • Bring down a structure as big as a house.
  • Be used as a weapon with tags ranged, area, brutal.
