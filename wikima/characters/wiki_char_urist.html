Origin

Urist is simple dwarf, oldest son of working class family, with six unwieldy brothers that were always up to some shenanigans usually lending them in trobles and Urist having to step in to help them out. For majority of his life, he has a clear record of honest no-bullshit foreman with impressive record, worst offence is occasional drunk skrimishes whiсh is common amoung his culture. After the first contact he lost his parents and wife to a ruin (later they was Reclaimed). He walked out of company with his brothers and friends and later was linked to some underground workings, mostly as hired muskles. It appeared he was approached by Fom and asked to join the cause and he agreed. There is a rumors that he used to help her with smuggling of rare ores and underground chemicals but it was not confirmed.

Moves


WORK HARD

When you assess a disaster, crisis, or hardship, name the inglorious task that can sort things out and roll +Roles marked. On a 7-9, you can gather your crew and perform the task, but choose two. On a +10, choose one:

  • It’s too complex. You will need support from a highly skilled artisan or scholar.
  • It’s too big. You’ll need to recruit dozens more strong arms.
  • It’s too dangerous. Your crew might not survive it.
  • It’s too costly. There’s a Surplus you must expend to succeed.

AS ONE

Your reputation draws followers from far and wide: your starting Companions gain the Horde and Hardy tags. When a new Cycle starts, your crew gets larger – gain new Companions with a free tag, in addition to Horde and Hardy.

110%

You can decide to overcharge a piece of your equipment to perform beyond its safe settings. You get advantage on a next roll using that piece of tech, but choose one.

  • It goes haywire, affecting far more than you desired.
  • It’s damaged and must be repaired in a place of safety.
  • Its effect turns on you, leaving you with lingering physical trauma.
