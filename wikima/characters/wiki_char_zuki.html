Appearence

Unremarkable in colour and foliage, Zuki's brown and yellow make him a drab-looking Floran. He is usually seen carrying a pair of spears, but judging from his voiced complaints, it's clear that Zuki wants to play with guns. This strong, impulsive Floran is a quick thinker, but even quicker on his feet.

Origin

Zuki was one of the first laboratory raised Florans of the Ecologist team and one of the few candidates to show any skill in ballistics. He was thusly thrust into a security role before the whole Floran incident.
