Major Mackendrick

Colony Administration

Appearence

Despite having a body of a veteran, stands stiff and strong. In public often seen wearing colony militia uniform with a green beret. Under the uniform his body is littered with plethora of prosthetics and dermal plating, thought his face remained intact.
