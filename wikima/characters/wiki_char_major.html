Major Mackendrick

Colony Administration

Appearence

Despite having a body of a veteran, stands stiff and strong. In public often seen wearing colony militia uniform with a green beret

Origin

Retired army Major, got rehired by a private security company "Wilson Security" that got assimilated into Transtar not long after. After a long time jumping from one post to another finally ended up getting placed as a colony militia chief. His loyalty gives him a comfortable life and a respected position, but having seen conflicts in three different sectors during the span of his career he treats it as a job, not as a duty.

Assets

  • Service battle dress (Armored, Uniform, Utility)
  • Heavy kinetic pistol (Ranged, Hefty, Piercing)
  • IFV (Land, Durable, Mighty)
  • SFiR team (Drilled, Vicious, Shadowy)
