Floran Tribes

Force 0 | Lore -1 | Ssssteel +2 | Sway +1

Appearence

Being one of the saplings brought into the Druid program in the Neutral Grounds to learn about the Druids and their ways, this Floran was among the most promising candidates for learning magic from the indigenous population, until she got hold of some crayons and caused a minor calamity that left several other Florans crippled and even more humans scarred for life.

Origin

This ditzy, beet-red Floran both in skin and foliage is often seen frolicking about in a muddled manner, with no specific cause or pattern to her actions. While followed around by an escort of warriors, Ulululu seems to have a short attention span, often forgetting what she was doing or becoming blissfully unaware of what's happening around her. Despite this, she is quite resourceful, or lucky. One of the two...

Moves


MY WAY

Whenever you are given advice by a wise or experienced character, you can instead shrug them off, take +1 on pursuing your own idea. They will know about it later.

FACE OF INNOCENCE

When you are the target of someone's aggressive or violent behaviour, roll+Sway. On a 10+, they may not harm, punish, or humiliate you. On a 7-9, they may choose 1:

  • Inflict minor physical discomfort.
  • Take something from you.
  • Humiliate you, giving you -1 with them until you even the score.
